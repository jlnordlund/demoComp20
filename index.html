Welcome to the online remote control for your SwarmBot 1.0

The SwarmBot was created in order to provide a cohesive entertainment system for children from ages 7-12 during the boring times of the COVID19 pandemic.

The SwarmBot comes equipped with multiple modes including the game mode activated by the buttons on your bot and the Line Following mode that can be activated from the Line Following page.

In order to manually control your bot, feel free to play around on the Remote control page!
